Frying is a dry heat coooking method where the raw food item is submerged in hot oil or fat. Common examples include french fries, fried chicken etc.


Baking is a method of cooking that uses dry heat, typically in an oven, to prepare food. It is commonly used for making bread, cakes, pastries, and other baked goods.


Prepping ingredients involves washing, chopping, measuring, and organizing all the necessary components before cooking or baking. This step ensures a smooth cooking process and helps in achieving better results.


Plating and presentation refer to the art of arranging and decorating food on a plate to enhance its visual appeal. A well-presented dish can elevate the dining experience and make the food more appetizing.
