About Me

Hello! My name is Adeniran Adelosoye. I got into cooking and baking around 5 years ago (2020) when I was locked up at home during the pandemic. Since then, I've developed a passion for creating delicious meals and treats. I first developed intrest in it because my sister used to make tasty dishes and desserts that I really enjoyed. Over the years, I've experimented with various recipes and techniques, and I love sharing my culinary creations with friends and family. Cooking and baking have become a way for me to express my creativity and unwind after a long day. Although I'm still learning, I'm excited to continue improving my skills and exploring new flavors in the kitchen. Some of my favorite dishes to make include homemade Jollof rice, meatpie, banana bread, chin chins, and cookies.
