Cooking and Baking

Cooking

Cooking is the process of preparing food by combining, mixing, and heating ingredients to make them safe to eat and enjoyable. It includes different methods such as boiling, frying, steaming, roasting, and grilling. Cooking helps bring out the flavors, textures, and nutrients in food. It can be a creative and satisfying activity, allowing individuals to experiment with different recipes and cuisines. Cooking at home also promotes healthier eating habits and can be a fun way to bond with family and friends over shared meals.

Baking

Baking is a method of cooking that uses dry heat, usually in an oven, to prepare food. It is commonly used to make items like bread, cakes, pies, and pastries. Baking requires precise measurements and timing to achieve the right texture and taste.
